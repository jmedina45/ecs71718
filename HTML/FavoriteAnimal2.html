They live primarily along rivers within thick scrubby vegetation. Some live in savannas and open woodlands, marshes and sometimes grasslands, but not in open areas. They are also hunters, and will eat a wide variety of animals, mainly birds, fish, reptiles, amphibians, wild guinea pigs, rodents, hares, small agoutis, and other small mammals.
