I went to Philip Rogers Elementary for 10 years. I began
my journey at the school when I was in pre-school at just
6 years old, and recently graduated when I was 14. Being with
just about the same group of people for a decade was the greatest
experience, and I couldn't have been happier.


I have had many academic accomplishments while attending Phillip
Rogers Elementary. From pre-school all the way to my 8th grade
graduation, I had only gotten As, no Bs, Cs, etc. I am very humbled
by that accomplishment. I also won science fair two years in a row,
7th and 8th grade. I also got one of the highest math MAP scores
(on record) that year.
